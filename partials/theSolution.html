THE SOLUTION
Reimagining the marketplace
We are partnering directly with healthcare service providers to list service pricing and terms upfront on our app. Users are able to select which services they would like, and directly connect to the corresponding provider. With our process in place, the user and the provider knows all the terms of servicing and everyone walks away happy.
Price Transparency
For many, going to the doctor is a gamble. It's unclear what will end
up on your bill, or how much it might cost. BitClaims changes all of
that. With services priced up front, patients are empowered to make
informed healthcare choices. Our app makes it easy to compare
services, so that from the start, you know the terms and the price.
BitClaims demystifies the healthcare market, allowing you to shop
for care like you would any other service.
